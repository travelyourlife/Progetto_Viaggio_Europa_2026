Privacy Policy — Quo Vadis

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Overview

Quo Vadis is a private travel companion app designed for family use during a European road trip. The app is not intended for general public use and is accessible only to invited family members and approved followers.

Data We Collect

The app collects the following data exclusively to provide its core functionality:

  • Authentication data: Google account email and display name (via Firebase Authentication) — used to identify users and manage access permissions.
  • Location data (optional): GPS coordinates — used only for the Live Map feature to share real-time position with family members. Location sharing is entirely voluntary and can be disabled at any time.
  • Push notification tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens — used to deliver trip updates, chat messages, and reminders.
  • Chat messages: Text messages sent in the family chat — stored in Firebase Realtime Database and visible only to approved family members.
  • Diary entries: Travel diary posts created by the trip owner — shared with approved followers.

How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide trip information and real-time updates to family members
  • Enable communication between family members via chat
  • Share live location during the trip
  • Send push notifications about trip events and reminders

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Data is shared only among approved family members within the app.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Realtime Database) servers located in the EU (europe-west1). Data is protected by Firebase's built-in security rules and is accessible only to authenticated and authorized users.

Data Retention

Data is retained for the duration of the trip and a reasonable period afterward for memories and reference. Users can request deletion of their data at any time by contacting the app administrator.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Database, Cloud Messaging, Hosting
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage statistics (disabled for family members/owner)
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — Map tiles and routing

Children's Privacy

This app is a private family app. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent. Family members of all ages may use the app under parental supervision.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for location sharing at any time
  • Request removal of your account from the app

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact the app administrator at: travelyourlife@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
