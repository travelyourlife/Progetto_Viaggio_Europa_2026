## Issues Found - Finland
1. Tree species percentages (41%/38%) are from FAO volume data, not area. Acceptable since FAO is cited.
2. "31 species" → change to "circa 30 specie" to be safe
3. Lady's slipper "fino a 10 cm" → change to "6-8 cm" for accuracy
4. Wolf population may need note about growth

## Overall Finland: MOSTLY ACCURATE - minor fixes needed
